Favorite Breeds: Siberian & Ragdoll

Siberian Cat

The Siberian is a beautiful, robust cat breed known for its thick, triple-layered coat and playful nature. Originating from Russia, this breed is highly adaptable and can handle colder climates due to its heavy fur.

Unique Traits:

  • Size: Medium to large, muscular build
  • Coat: Long, thick, and water-repellent
  • Personality: Affectionate, energetic, and social
  • Fun Fact: Siberians are known for their dog-like loyalty and often form strong bonds with their owners.

Ragdoll Cat

The Ragdoll is a large, fluffy cat breed known for its calm and relaxed demeanor. They are often described as "puppy-like" because they enjoy following their owners around and being handled. This breed is very affectionate and enjoys snuggling.

Unique Traits:

  • Size: Large, with a soft, plush coat
  • Coat: Semi-longhair with a silky texture
  • Personality: Gentle, relaxed, and loving
  • Fun Fact: Ragdolls are known for going limp when picked up, hence their name.
